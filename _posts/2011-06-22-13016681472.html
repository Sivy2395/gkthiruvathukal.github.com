--- layout: post title: Digital Humanities/CS 2011 CFP now available excerpt: The conference has come and gone, but you can check out the proceedings at chicagocolloquium.org. --- Digital Humanities/CS 2011 CFP now available

We’re hosting the Chicago Colloquium on Digital Humanities and Computer Science at Loyola University Chicago this year. While the name “Chicago” is in the title, the conference has historically attracted submissions from around the country and the world. 


This will be the first offering at Loyola University Chicago and is on Chicago’s Magnificent Mile and lakefront.  I look forward to seeing you there.
